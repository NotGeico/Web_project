Portfolio

Welcome to my portfolio! Here, you'll find a showcase of some of the projects I’ve worked on in the past, highlighting my skills and creativity as a designer and editor. Among the various tools I use, I feel most proficient in Adobe Illustrator and Premiere Pro, which is why I’ve chosen to feature examples of my work created using these programs. Illustrator allows me to craft visually appealing designs, such as logos and graphics, while Premiere Pro helps me bring stories to life through dynamic video editing.

If you’re interested in seeing more of my Premiere Pro expertise, I also create content for my YouTube channel, where I share entertaining and well-edited gaming videos. Although I’ve only uploaded one video so far, I’m excited about posting more in the future as I continue to grow and refine my channel.

In addition to my proficiency with Illustrator and Premiere Pro, I’m also skilled in Photoshop and InDesign, two powerful tools that allow me to expand my creative capabilities. Photoshop helps me retouch and manipulate images with precision, while InDesign enables me to design professional layouts for brochures, magazines, and other print materials. I take pride in my versatility and dedication to honing my craft across these Adobe platforms, and I’m eager to demonstrate my abilities further. Feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out if you’d like to collaborate or learn more about my work!
